Welcome to Volleyball Pexinois Niort

Volleyball Pexinois Niort is a dynamic and inclusive volleyball club located in Niort, France. We offer a range of opportunities for players of all ages and skill levels to learn, play, and enjoy the sport of volleyball.

Programs and Teams

We have programs for everyone, including youth development leagues, recreational leagues, and competitive teams. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, we have a team for you.

Facilities and Events

Our facilities include indoor and outdoor courts, training equipment, and changing rooms. We also host regular events, such as tournaments, clinics, and social gatherings, for our members and the wider community.

Join Our Club

If you're interested in joining our club, please contact us at contact@volleyballpexinoisniort.fr for more information. We look forward to welcoming you to Volleyball Pexinois Niort!
